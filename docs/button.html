button

The atom-button component is a themed and atom-state-integrated UI button with (optional) icons.

@Param: label - the text to display on the button
@Param: variant - (optional) its visual variant (normal | primary | destructive | constructive | subtle | outline)
@Param: onclick - (optional) the method to call when clicked
